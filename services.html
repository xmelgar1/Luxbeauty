Eyelash Extensions

Eyelash extensions are semipermanent lashes that are hand-glued on top of your natural lashes and unlike strip lashes, lash extensions are glued on individually to your natural hairs, they are customizable and look super real.

How Long will a set of Eyelash Extensions last?

When applied by a professional, eyelash extensions will last the length of the natural growth cycle of your own eyelashes, refills should only be required every 2 to 4 weeks.

How long will it take to apply Eyelash Extensions?

The application process for lash extensions normally takes about 90-120 minutes for a full set of lashes and 45-60 minutes for a refill. You will lay comfortably on a massage table with your eyes closed while relaxing ambient music is played in the background. During application, your lower lashes will be covered and the extensions will be applied only to your upper lashes one at a time.

Eyelash Extensions Types

Classic Lashes

Classic lashes means there's one single extension attached to one single, natural lash. If you want more volume, the lash technician can apply a few extension hairs to a single, natural lash, which is commonly called a fan lash or volume lash.

Hybrid Lashes

Hybrid lashes are a mixture of both classic and volume lashes. Choosing this option will give you the best of both worlds. This look is achieved by mixing classic lashes and volume lashes strategically placed to frame the eyes.

Volume Lashes

Volume lashes are pre-made fans or the fans can be made by hand using a special tweezer. The fans are still attached to the natural lashes and have more fluff and volume, for a more dramatic look
